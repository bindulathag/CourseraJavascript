Classification of Plants

Plants are classified into several types based on the basis of their growth habit. Explore more about - herbs, shrubs, trees, climbers and creepers

Plants Food

Their roots take up water and minerals from the ground and their leaves absorb a gas called carbon dioxide (CO2) from the air. They convert these ingredients into food by using energy from sunlight. This process is called photosynthesis, which means 'making out of light'. The foods are called glucose and starch.

Parts of Plants

The different parts of plants include the roots, stem, leaves, flowers, and fruits. Every part works in coordination and performs various functions.
